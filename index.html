Hi, I'm Harvey McCabe

A Digital Strategist and Senior Content Lead based in Manchester, UK.

Newly freelance, I combine canny insights with creativity to solve strategic problems and build lively communities. From perception shifting organic social, to long-term audience growth, and storytelling through video and podcasts.

I have over twelve years' experience of building communities and campaigns for brands in the UK audio industry (including the BBC and Bauer Media) - until recently, working with my extremely talented team at BBC Radio 2, Radio 3 and 6 Music to deliver 1B+ organic video views as their Senior Social Media Lead.

Key projects included:

  • Coverage of flagship BBC live music events (Glastonbury, Radio 2 in the Park, 6 Music Festival, BBC Proms - all delivering record numbers)
  • Growing streaming audiences for Radio 2 and 6 Music, with a repositioning of their social offer
  • Proposing and launching a modern new presence for Radio 3, inviting new audiences to access classical music on platforms like TikTok
  • 6 Music's ‘Change The Tune’ initiative – addressing online abuse towards musicians.

Before that, as BBC Radio Social's Senior Audiences Planner, I led a transformed approach to data and key work around project prioritisation and platform tactics - collaborating with editorial, audience, production and marketing teams across all national networks.

I'm experienced in working with a wide variety of niche audiences - translating social reach into measurable success against business objectives. Other clients have included BBC Radio 1, Monty Digital, S:E Creative Studio, Greatest Hits Radio and Hits Radio, and Audio Always.

Most of all, I enjoy bringing together teams to deliver content that offers genuine value to its audience - prioritising open and empathetic leadership.
