Hi, I’m Harvey. A digital culture obsessive who probably spends a bit too much time on the internet.

Digital leader, with a background in audio and entertainment.

Manchester, UK.

Here to work on projects with purpose - and to deliver value to audiences with creative, collaborative, and empathetic leadership.

I have over a decade of experience leading digital brands and campaigns in the UK audio and music industry – including roles at BBC Sounds (a combined seven years, spanning all their national stations - including Radio 1, Radio 2, and Radio 4) and Bauer Media (Heat, Greatest Hits Radio, Hits Radio) – starting my career as a radio producer before specialising in social media.

After taking a career break to travel around Australia, I’m excited to pursue a career pivot - applying my skills to a Product Owner/Delivery Manager type role in the tech space. I am currently studying for my Professional Scrum Product Owner I (PSPO I) Certification.

My background brings together creative and brand strategy, digital growth, and a lifelong passion for technology - backed by solid experience of agile ways of working and bridging cross-functional teams.

Most recently, I was the Senior Social Media Lead for BBC Radio 2, Radio 3 and 6 Music – overseeing creative output for the three public service networks, shaping strategy with stakeholders, and empowering my extremely talented team to deliver over 1 billion organic video views in 2024, which contributed to strong YoY growth on the BBC Sounds platform.

Key projects included:

  • Coverage of flagship BBC live music events (Glastonbury Festival, Radio 2 in the Park, 6 Music Festival, BBC Proms - all delivering record numbers)
  • Successful editorial and platform strategy refreshes for Radio 2 and 6 Music
  • Proposing and launching a modern new presence for the BBC's classical music output
  • 6 Music's ‘Change The Tune’ moderation initiative – aimed at making online spaces more positive for musicians and fans.

Before that, I was BBC Radio Social's Senior Planner, leading portfolio-wide projects, establishing transparent processes for prioritisation and performance measurement, developing and testing tactics, and building valuable collaborations across the organisation.

Whether it’s managing delivery of digital projects, leading change with technical and business/editorial teams, or shaping and communicating strategy, I'd love to chat about where I could fit into your team.
