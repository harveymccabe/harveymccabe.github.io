Hi, I'm Harvey - a digital strategist and creative lead that probably spends too much time on the internet.

Manchester, UK.

Passionate about digital culture and using tech for public good. Collaborative and empathetic leadership for projects with purpose.

I have over a decade of experience leading social communities and campaigns for brands in the UK audio industry (BBC and Bauer Media) - most recently, working with my extremely talented team to deliver 1B+ organic video views as Senior Social Media Lead for BBC Radio 2, Radio 3 and 6 Music.

Key projects included:

  • 🎪 Coverage of flagship BBC live music events (Glastonbury Festival, Radio 2 in the Park, 6 Music Festival, BBC Proms - all delivering record numbers)
  • 📲 Growing audiences for Radio 2 and 6 Music, with refreshes of their social offer
  • 🎻 Proposing and launching a modern new presence for Radio 3, inviting new audiences to access classical music on platforms like TikTok
  • 🎵 6 Music's ‘Change The Tune’ social action initiative – addressing the issue of online abuse towards musicians.

Before that, as BBC Radio Social's Senior Planner, I led a transformed approach to data and key work around project prioritisation and platform tactics - collaborating with editorial, audience insight, production and marketing teams.

My background brings together creative strategy, audience growth, and a life-long love of tech.

After taking a career break to travel around Australia, I’m now looking for new opportunities in the digital and tech product spaces. I am currently studying for my Professional Scrum Product Owner (PSPO I) Certification.

Whether it’s crafting strategies that bring genuine value to brands and audiences, using data and insight to find answers, developing campaigns that cut through the noise, or leading change with business teams, I'd love to chat about where I could fit into your work.
